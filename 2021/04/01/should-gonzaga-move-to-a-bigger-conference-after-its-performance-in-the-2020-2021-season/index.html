library(ggrepel)

    )

This shows my conclusion, Gonzaga is really good at offense and defense along with USC and Arizona. This is with an exception of Colorado and UCLA. Oregon, Oregon St., Washington St and exception of California are good defensively but not on offense while Stanford and Utah can’t execute on either side of the ball. In my personal conclusion I think Gonzaga would fit better in the Mountain West due to the weak strength of schedule in the PAC-12.
